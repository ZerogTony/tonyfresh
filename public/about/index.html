Turning Tide

Company London Festival of Architecture

Role Designer

Practice Graphic Design

The turning tide was a conceptual bench designed as part of a national competition run by the London festival of Architecture. I and my brother collaborated on the winning design which we were then commissioned to build and install within a month.The theme of the design was "act". Our design interpreted this theme by focusing on the organic transition between mental states, and symbolising the transformative power of rest and reflection on the mind. We wanted the design to portray a fluid change in thought, often the precursor to an act. To connect this idea with the community, we incorporated cages on the sides of the bench, allowing park-goers to leave messages on rocks for future visitors.
